SpatialDI 2024

The Spatial Data Intelligence China Conference is committed to promoting the research paradigm of spatial data and the innovation and application of spatial intelligence theory and technology in the fields of spatiotemporal big data, smart cities, transportation science, social governance, and so on. In order to further facilitate the theoretical development and application of spatial data intelligence research, and to exchange new theories, new problems and new methods in related fields, the 5-th China Conference of Spatial Data Intelligence (SpatialDI 2024) will be held in Nanjing from April 25-27.
